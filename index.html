Why DublinDabba

Food That Feels Like Home

Home-Cooked Daily

Prepared fresh each morning by local Dublin families using traditional recipes passed down through generations. Never batch-frozen. Never reheated.

Wholesome Ingredients

No artificial colours, flavours or preservatives. We source seasonal vegetables from trusted Irish-Indian grocery partners. What you see is what you eat.

Lunchtime Delivery

Delivered to your door or office between 11:30 AM and 1:30 PM, Monday to Saturday. Hot, sealed, and ready to enjoy — right on time.

100% Dublin-Made

From our kitchen to your door — all within Dublin. We cover Dublin 1–24, city centre and suburbs. Same-day delivery, no delays, no middle-men.

The Process

Three Steps to Happiness

01

Choose Your Plan

Pick daily, weekly, or monthly. Select vegetarian, non-veg, or Jain. Choose your delivery slot — morning or lunchtime. Completely flexible.

02

We Cook Fresh

Every morning our Dublin kitchen preps your tiffin from scratch — grounded spices, fresh vegetables, slow-cooked curries. Packed hot and sealed airtight.

Your dabba arrives by 1 PM. Leave feedback via WhatsApp — we adjust to your spice level, portion preference, and dietary needs. Every time.
